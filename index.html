Welcome

This website aims to serve as an introduction and general summary to the Men’s Rights Movement. Both men and women who care about equality are joining the movement and are helping to raise awareness about the legal and social issues that impact boys and men. Egalitarian in nature, the Men’s Rights Movement advocates equality for all genders and sexes, making it a true civil rights movement. Men’s Rights Advocates around the world are joining forces to give men and boys a voice!
